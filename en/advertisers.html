Emerged form Otaniemi, Espoo, Radiodiodi is 100% student-made, commercial but non-profit radio station. The 2017's Radiodiodi broadcast will be the sixth one, in April 18 to 30. Every year dozens of students does immense amount of work to get the broadcast on-air. Round-the-clock broadcasts are hosted by almost a hundred program hosts!

This all means that the collaboration between Radiodiodi and companies of all industries is extremely vital to our operations. Without sponsors the most awesome student radio broadcast simply wouldn't be possible to execute.

Media Info

  • Essential part of the 1st of May time of the technology students in Finland!
  • Listeners of FM broadcast mainly from Otaniemi but it is proven that other students in Helsinki area listens our broadcast as well as already graduated people.
  • Radiodiodi is played non-stop in break rooms and canteens around Otaniemi Campus Area.
  • Our webstreams number of simultaneous listeners has peaked previously in about 300. So far we have no data about the FM-listeners.
  • Radiodiodi is a rising porject: during the year 2016 our Facebooks' likes alone rised 40%
  • In 2017 our broadcast will be marketed more widely outside Otaniemi.

Sets

Our sponsors and parters are higly visible and audible in Otaniemi during the 1st of May time. Our studio is in a cargo container in the middle of all happenings, in Alvari Square. In Otaniemi, our broadcast is extremely hard to miss.

  • Post made in accordance with the wishes of the partner to our social media channels.
  • Partners logo placed in our website.

Price for commercial MAX 22snt/minute

  • Same as in partnership, but the commercial played 30 times a day.

  • Chance ot bring your promotional mterials (pens, caps, etc.) to be shared from our containers in Alvari Square.
  • Visibility in all of our releases and publications (banners all around Otaniemi, info material, etc).
