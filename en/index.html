Radiodiodi, made from Espoo's Otaniemi is a Finnish non-profit student radio project. Broadcasted nationwide through the last two weeks of April 2017 the student based radio program makes whole Helsinki area elation for the 6th times in a row. Every year dozens of student volunteers accross Helsinki area carry out all the background work. And then there are almost one hundred actual program hosts. The whole Helsinki area can listen our program. There lives almost 400 000 people in our calculated coverage area!

Yrityksille

Radiodiodi is commercial but non-profit radio station. Cooperation and partnership with all kinds of companies is therefore vital to our operations. Our project can be supported by various ways: money, material support or almost anything radio-related (or not) is happily accepted!

Interested in being a part of this? Want to learn to use modern sound technology? Intrigued by participating in the awesome team with neverending fun? Radiodiodi offers easy solution for all these: become a part of us! Dozens of people with every kind of competence are needed. Free-form applications are always accepted, more info below!

Producers takes care of the production of commercials and the actual radio programs. Producers ensure the program hosts the possibility to focus fully to the program itself, without the connsern of faders and levels etc. We grant a personal producer for every program host if they don't want to produce themselves. Producers are also responsible for the availability of the music that hosts wants to play in their show. Producing commercials means figuring the idea, recording and editing. The Chief of producers is the Editor in Chief.
Studio technicians are responsible for the operation and nostruction of the studio. Devices and technology are built and refined in good time prior to the execution of the broadcast. When the containers arrive the only thing to be made is the construciton. In the lead of Studio technicians is the Chief of Studio.
IT-people keeps the complicated IT infrastrucutre in control. Construction and maintenance of a proper IT system is vital for Radiodiodi's production-capability. Everything must stay up and running continuously without exception. The management of the IT department is in the hands of IT-manager.
Broadcast Technicians are small but life-and-death important part of the project. The actual broadcasting is doubtlessly important part of Radiodiodi. Their territory consists of FM and web broadcasts construction and maintenance. Chief of Broadcast in the lead.
Marketing and visibility are handled by the Media Team. Useful skills for the operations are lightning-fast skills in GIMP, photography and sound editing. Secretary of the Board manages the Media Team.
Promoters sell radio commercials and negotiate great sponsorship deals. They are in charge of the company relations in a wide scale. This is managed by our CEO.

How to get along?

Inspired? Intrigued? Express your feelings towards the job you are interested in by mailing us a short free-form description of yourslef to board@radiodiodi.fi ! All other questions are also welcom via that mail or our Telegram channel !

However, please note a few things:

  • In Radiodiodi we use Slack in our internal communications. using this platform is a prerequisite for participation.
  • Even though big part of the project is pre-made throughout the year, the workload in April will be huge. Freshmen are welcome to participate of course, but keep in mind that we count on your dedication even though the sixth beer-pong -tournament is fun ;)
  • Also, if you happen to be interested in hosting something bigger radio program, morning show for example, be in touch!
  • Didn't find anything particularly interesting above? Have something else in your maing? Or just want to be part of our awesome team, be in touch! As always, work will be for fine and motivated people.
